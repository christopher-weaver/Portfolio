About

Throughout my ten-year career in the financial services industry, I was always looking for opportunities to implement technology in order to help my team function more efficiently. Initially self-taught in coding, I often had to get creative and work within the technolgical constraints of the business, implementing countless solutions that utilized Visual Basic for Applications (VBA).

In 2020, I had the opportunity to do some consulting for a friend's business. This work started off in VBA, but I quickly realized that I no longer had to work under my previous constraints. I began brushing up on my C# and Javascript skills to better meet the needs of the business, and in 2021, I enrolled in the part-time software development program at Eleven Fifty Academy to broaden my skill set.

Let's work together to creatively solve the most challenging aspects of your business.
